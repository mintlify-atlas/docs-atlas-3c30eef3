## Overview

The Foundations Track (Weeks 1-8) builds your understanding from mathematical first principles to complete transformer architectures. By the end of Week 8, you'll understand how modern language models are constructed, trained, and scaled.

<Info>
  **Time Commitment**: 8 weeks at 10-15 hours per week
  
  **Prerequisites**: Basic Python programming, high school algebra
</Info>

## Learning Arc

The first eight weeks follow a deliberate progression:

1. **Mathematical Foundations** (Weeks 1-2): Build intuition for optimization and linear algebra
2. **Token Representations** (Week 3): Understand how text becomes vectors
3. **Attention Mechanics** (Week 4): Master the core innovation of transformers
4. **Architecture Assembly** (Week 5): Compose complete transformer blocks
5. **Training Fundamentals** (Weeks 6-7): Learn loss functions, regularization, and generalization
6. **Scaling Principles** (Week 8): Understand empirical scaling laws and system design

## Pedagogy Approach

Every week in the Foundations Track follows the rigorous **6-point math standard**:

1. Plain-language intuition
2. Complete symbol glossary
3. Step-by-step derivation (no skipped algebra)
4. Visual graphs and diagrams
5. Toy numeric walkthrough
6. Direct formula-to-code mapping

This ensures you build **deep understanding**, not surface-level familiarity.
